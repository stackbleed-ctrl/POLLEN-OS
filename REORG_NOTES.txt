This zip was reorganized into a standard Android app tree.

- Moved root-level Kotlin sources into app/src/main/kotlin/... based on each file's declared package.
- Moved root XML resources into app/src/main/res/... and AndroidManifest.xml into app/src/main/.
- Moved Android CI workflow to .github/workflows/android.yml.
- Removed duplicate flat root source/resource files to avoid two competing project layouts.
